About the Conference

SCOPE OF THE CONFERENCE

The field of mechanical engineering continues to evolve rapidly, driven by advancements in technology, materials science, and innovative design techniques. With the growing demand for sustainable solutions, automation, and cutting-edge manufacturing processes, it is essential to create a platform that brings together experts, researchers, and practitioners to exchange knowledge and collaborate on the future of mechanical engineering.

  • To provide a platform for researchers, engineers, and industry professionals to discuss recent developments and challenges in mechanical engineering.
  • To promote the exchange of ideas and innovations in mechanical design, automation, robotics, material science, energy systems, and manufacturing technologies.
  • To facilitate networking opportunities that could lead to future collaborations, partnerships, and research initiatives.
  • To address key global issues such as sustainability, energy efficiency, and automation through the lens of mechanical engineering.

Target Audience

The event is designed to bring together a diverse group of participants, including academicians, researchers, and students from universities and research institutions who are engaged in advancing knowledge in mechanical engineering. It also welcomes industry professionals, engineers, and technologists from key sectors such as automotive, aerospace, energy, robotics, manufacturing, and thermal engineering.

  • Academicians, researchers, and students from universities and research institutions.
  • Industry professionals, engineers, and technologists from various sectors, including automotive, aerospace, energy, robotics, manufacturing and thermal engineering.
  • Policy makers, government officials, and corporate executives with an interest in mechanical engineering innovations.
  • Anyone interested in exploring the latest trends and challenges in the field

ABOUT PRAYAGRAJ
THE SANGAM CITY

Prayagraj, formerly known as Allahabad, is a major city in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. It is headquarter of the Prayagraj district and is a major pilgrimage center for Hindus. Prayagraj is a renowned spiritual city where Kumbh Mela is held every twelve years in which crores of devotees from different corners of the world come to take a dip of faith in the holy Triveni Sangam of Ganga, Yamuna and Saraswati.

Key Themes and Topics

Track-1: Material Synthesis for environmental remediation, Energy storage and Conversion

Synthesis and characterization of materials, innovation and mechanistic features for sustainable chemical production, nanomaterials for environmental remediation, Advanced Oxidation processes, Conversion of CO2 into valuable chemicals, Energy Harvesting, Battery Technology, Supercapacitors, Solar Energy Conversion, Hydrogen Storage, Catalysis for Energy Applications.

Track-2: Advance Manufacturing in Industry 4.0/5.0

Nanofabrication, Additive Manufacturing, 3D Printing, Smart Manufacturing, Process Optimization, Materials Processing, Quality Control, Data-Driven Manufacturing, Sustainable Manufacturing, Composites, Advanced Welding Technologies, Application of AI/ML in Manufacturing.

Track-3: Thermal Engineering

Combustion, Multiphase flow, Fluid mechanics, Heat and Mass transfer, Nano fluids thermal applications, Refrigeration, Solar energy, CFD, Computational heat transfer, Heat exchanger, Phase change materials, Renewable energy, Thermal management.

Track-4: Energy Development and Power Generation

Advanced and renewable energy technologies, Power system stabilizers, Modelling and simulation of power plants, Solar/photovoltaic, Wind, Biomass, Batteries, Fuel cells, Distributed storage, Grid interconnection, Tidal/wave power, Industrial energy conservation.
